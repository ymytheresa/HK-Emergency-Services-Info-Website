醫院詳情 | Hospital Details

港怡醫院是港島區唯一設有24小時急症室的私家醫院，由急症科專科醫生主理。醫院設有完善的急症及專科服務，為患者提供優質的私家醫療服務。

Gleneagles Hospital Hong Kong is the only private hospital on Hong Kong Island with a 24-hour A&E, run by Emergency Medicine Specialists. The hospital offers comprehensive emergency and specialist services, providing quality private healthcare.
