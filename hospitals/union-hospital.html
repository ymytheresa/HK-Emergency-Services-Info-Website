醫院詳情 | Hospital Details

仁安醫院是新界區首間獲認可的私家急症科中心，由急症科專科醫生主理。醫院設有24小時心臟病及中風急救服務，配備先進的醫療設備和專業醫療團隊。

Union Hospital is the first recognized private Emergency Medicine Centre in the New Territories, run by Emergency Medicine Specialists. The hospital offers 24-hour cardiac and stroke rescue services with advanced medical equipment and professional medical teams.
