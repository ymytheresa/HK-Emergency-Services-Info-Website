醫院詳情 | Hospital Details

瑪嘉烈醫院為九龍西聯網的主要創傷中心，提供24小時骨科服務。醫院設有完善的急症科及創傷處理設施，是九龍西區處理嚴重創傷的主要醫院。

Princess Margaret Hospital is the major trauma center for the Kowloon West Cluster, providing 24-hour orthopedic services. The hospital has comprehensive emergency and trauma facilities, serving as the main hospital for severe trauma cases in West Kowloon.
