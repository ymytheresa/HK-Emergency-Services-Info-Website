醫院詳情 | Hospital Details

瑪麗醫院為港島西聯網的主要醫院及教學醫院，提供24小時急症室服務。作為香港大學醫學院的教學醫院，設有完善的急症及專科服務。

Queen Mary Hospital is the major hospital and teaching hospital for the Hong Kong West Cluster, providing 24-hour A&E services. As a teaching hospital of the University of Hong Kong Faculty of Medicine, it offers comprehensive emergency and specialist services.
